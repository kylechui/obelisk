- [[Generalized Chinese Remainder Theorem]]
- **Theorem**: Let $R$ be a ring, with $I, J\subseteq R$ [[Ideal|ideals]].
  Suppose $I + J = R$. Then we have the following isomorphism:
  $(R / I)\times (R / J)\cong R / (I\cap J)$.
